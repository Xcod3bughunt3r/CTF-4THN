### Scytcrypto - Crypto, 50pts

##Problem

Description: Decrypt this strange word: ERTKSOOTCMCHYRAFYLIPL


## Solution

ERTKSOOTCMCHYRAFYLIPL

Starting from first sign, we have to use padding equals 3 signs.

1st row:

E  K  O  M  Y  F  I

2nd row:

 R  S  T  C  R  Y  P

3rd row:

  T  O  C  H  A  L  L
  
flag: EKO{MYFIRSTCRYPTOCHALL}
